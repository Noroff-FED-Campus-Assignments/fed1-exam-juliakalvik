HI! I'M JULIA

Welcome to my wedding photography blog! I'm a 27-year-old wedding photographer with a passion for capturing love and natural moments. With about 7 years of experience shooting weddings, I have had the honor of documenting countless love stories and creating timeless memories for my clients.

As your wedding photographer, I believe that my role is to not only document the story of your special day - but also to capture the love and connection between you as a couple. Whether it's candid shots of your guests, or intimate portraits of you and your partner, my goal is to create a visual narrative that tells the story of your wedding day, while also capturing the beauty and emotion of your love.

I understand that your wedding day is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, and it's important to have fun and be present in the moment. That's why I strive to create a relaxed and enjoyable environment, where you can simply be yourselves and enjoy the day with your loved ones. Rest assured that I will be there to guide you through the process and capture every precious moment.
